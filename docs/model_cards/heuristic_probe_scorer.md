# Model Card: Heuristic Fluorescent Probe Scorer

**Version:** 0.6.0

## Intended use
Prioritize fluorescent probe concepts for xylazine detection research using transparent, rule-based components.

## Validation status
Not experimentally validated. Scores are prioritization hypotheses and should be checked against literature and bench experiments.

## Limitations
Does not predict quantum yield, binding constants, detection limits, metabolic behavior, or synthetic success. Component scores are interpretable heuristics.

## Safety notes
AZAI is for analytical chemistry, public health, forensic detection, and education. Do not use to optimize harmful delivery, abuse potential, or clandestine synthesis.
